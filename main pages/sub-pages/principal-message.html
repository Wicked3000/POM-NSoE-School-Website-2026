Principal's Message

TO GOD BE THE GLORY! Welcome to Port Moresby National School of Excellence Website! I am honoured to serve as the principal of the school, POMNSoE.

Our governance structure is designed to ensure transparency, strategic direction, and community involvement. The Governing Council headed by Ps. Charles Lapa- OBE, comprising representatives from government agencies, Higher Institution, community leaders, alumni of the school and private sector organizations, oversees the school's policies, strategic planning, and overall governance. This body plays a crucial role in ensuring that the school remains aligned with national development goals and community expectations.

As Principal, I am dedicated to leading a team that supports both staff and students in achieving excellence. Our exemplary Deputy Principals focus on academic and administrative matters, ensuring smooth operations and continuous improvement. We aim to provide leadership that fosters accountability, innovation, and community engagement.

Our benevolent Teaching staff are structured to provide specialized support across various subject areas. Our exceptionally supportive heads of department oversee curriculum delivery, mentorship, and teacher development, ensuring that our students receive the best possible education. We are proud to offer both the Standard Base Curriculum (SBC) and Science Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) curriculum, combining traditional and latest trends, innovations and academic knowledge with practical, hands-on experiences to stimulate curiosity and problem-solving skills.

We also focus on holistic development, including character building, leadership, and community service, to prepare students to be responsible citizens.

We are also privileged to have a Student Representative Council (SRC) led by Mr. Dominic Mara, a dedicated STEM student, and Miss Beatrice Kala, a humanities student, together with their supportive team of committed executives, prefects, class captains, all of whom are highly admired for their leadership qualities.

Not forgetting our dedicated ancillary staff who always help and support the school. Their commitment and hard work are vital to our success.

In line with our commitment to transparency and effective communication, I am happy to announce the launch of our new school website. This platform will serve as a vital communication tool, providing students, parents, staff, and stakeholders with up-to-date information on school activities, academic programs, events, and other important matters. The website will also showcase student achievements, share upcoming opportunities, and foster stronger community engagement. I would like to thank Ms. Jonswa Francis, the Head of the Technology Department, for her tireless effort and dedication in making this website possible.

I would also like to acknowledge Jathniel Raja Kivori, a Grade 12 STEM student (12.H), who sacrificed his time to develop this website under the guidance of his technology teacher, Miss Jonswa, with full support from the ICT Division of the Department of Education.

As we embark on this journey of educational excellence, I invite all stakeholders (students, parents, teachers, support staff and community members) to join us in supporting our vision and mission. Together, we can build a brighter future for Papua New Guinea, powered by innovation, knowledge, and a shared commitment to excellence.

Teddy Feria
