Administration

Our administration stands as the backbone of the institution, guiding operations, maintaining discipline, and ensuring that every system functions with precision. Leadership here is built on responsibility, structure, and accountability.

Administrative Structure

The institution operates through a structured leadership system, ensuring efficiency and clarity in decision-making. Each level carries defined responsibilities that contribute to the overall success of the school.

  • Principal: Overall leadership and direction
  • Deputy Principal: Academic and operational support
  • Heads of Departments: Subject and curriculum management
  • Administrative Staff: Daily operations and coordination
