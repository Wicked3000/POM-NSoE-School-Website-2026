Uniform Policy

The school uniform policy supports discipline, unity, and respect. Students are expected to wear the correct uniform daily and maintain a neat, professional appearance.

School Policies

Students must wear the approved school uniform at all times during school hours, on field trips, and at official school functions. Uniforms must be clean, in good repair, and worn correctly.

  • All shirts, blouses and polos must be tucked in neatly.
  • Socks must be the approved colour and length for the students year level.
  • Only authorised school badges, belts, and accessories are permitted.
  • Footwear must be closed, plain, and school-approved; sandals are not permitted.

Rules & Expectations

Uniform standards help create a focused learning environment. Students are expected to meet the following rules daily.

Dress Neatly

Uniforms must be clean, pressed and free from unauthorised markings.

Be Consistent

Uniform must be worn properly and consistently throughout the day.

Follow Guidance

Students should make corrections immediately if asked by staff.

Policy Enforcement

The school monitors uniform compliance every day. Non-compliance is managed through a clear, fair process.

  1. First instance: verbal reminder and expectation review.
  2. Second instance: parent/guardian contact and written warning.
  3. Repeated non-compliance: formal disciplinary follow-up.
